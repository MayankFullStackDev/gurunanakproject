About Our Institute

Building Future Leaders

Established in 1995, Guru nanak Institute of Engineering & Technology has been at the forefront of technical education for over two decades. We are committed to providing world-class engineering education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical application.

Our institute fosters innovation, creativity, and critical thinking among students, preparing them to tackle the challenges of tomorrow's technological landscape. With state-of-the-art infrastructure and experienced faculty, we ensure our graduates are industry-ready.

We believe in holistic development, encouraging students to participate in research, extracurricular activities, and community service, shaping them into well-rounded professionals and responsible citizens.
